The following section outlines all the UG/PG level courses I have been associated with as a part of my teaching assistants (TA) duties during my undergrad and grad studies. TA duties mainly involve following academic activities which are considered crucial for an academic and teaching training.

  1. Proctoring exams
  2. Assignment evaluations
  3. Conducting vivas/demos
  4. Conducting tutorials/help sessions
  5. Developing lecture materials
  6. Sometimes co-teaching the course too

Primarily, I have been interested in TAing for ML/AI and CompLing/CogSci courses and have been fortunate to work with many Professors across the Institutes.
