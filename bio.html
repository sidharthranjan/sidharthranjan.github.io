I am a PhD student at School of Information Technology, IIT Delhi under the supervison of Prof. Sumeet Agarwal and Prof. Rajakrishnan Rajkumar. Currently, I'm a Visiting Research Student (VSR) at Department of Humanities and Social Sciences, IISER Bhopal. My PhD work mainly focuses on Cognitive modeling of Hindi syntactic choice. I am broadly interested in the mixture of computational psycholinguistics, natural language processing, machine learning, and cognitive science.

Before starting my grad school, I worked for Honeywell Aerospace, Bangalore as Senior Engineer where I developed softwares for testing electrical hardwares in cockpit systems viz., communication devices, display screens etc., and conducted automated Test Coverage Analyses. I recieved Dual Degree, B.Tech in Electrical Engineering and M.Tech in Information and Communication Technology from IIT Delhi.
